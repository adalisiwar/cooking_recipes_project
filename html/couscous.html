Tunisian Couscous Recipe

A flavorful, hearty stew served with steamed couscous, featuring lamb, vegetables, and spices.

Ingredients

  • 500g couscous
  • 1kg lamb (or chicken), cut into chunks
  • 2 medium onions, chopped
  • 3 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 carrots, peeled and cut into chunks
  • 1 zucchini, cut into chunks
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp cumin, coriander, cinnamon, and turmeric
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions

1. In a large pot, heat olive oil and brown the lamb (or chicken). Add the onions, garlic, and tomatoes and cook until softened.

2. Add the carrots, zucchini, spices, and tomato paste. Stir everything together.

3. Pour in enough water to cover the ingredients. Simmer on low heat for 1-1.5 hours, until the meat is tender and the vegetables are cooked.

4. Meanwhile, steam the couscous in a couscousier (or a fine sieve over boiling water) for 10 minutes, then fluff with a fork.

5. Serve the couscous with the stew on top. Garnish with fresh coriander.

Vegan Mediterranean Salad Recipe

A fresh and flavorful salad with cucumbers, tomatoes, olives, and chickpeas, dressed with olive oil and lemon juice.

Ingredients

  • 1 cucumber, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup black olives
  • 1 can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

1. In a large bowl, combine the cucumber, tomatoes, red onion, olives, and chickpeas.

2. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper to make the dressing.

3. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss gently.

4. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve chilled or at room temperature.

5. Enjoy your refreshing Mediterranean salad!

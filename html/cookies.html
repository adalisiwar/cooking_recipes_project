Chocolate Chip Cookies

Crispy on the outside and soft on the inside, these chocolate chip cookies are a treat!

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

2. In a small bowl, mix flour and baking soda. Set aside.

3. In a large bowl, cream butter, sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy.

4. Add eggs and vanilla extract, mixing well.

5. Gradually add the dry ingredients, then fold in chocolate chips.

6. Drop tablespoon-sized balls of dough onto the prepared baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes.

7. Allow to cool on a wire rack before enjoying!

Vanilla Cupcakes

Delicious and fluffy vanilla cupcakes with a sweet frosting.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup milk

Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).

2. Cream the butter and sugar together, then add eggs one by one.

3. Mix in the flour and milk, and stir in vanilla extract.

4. Pour into cupcake liners and bake for 18-20 minutes.

5. Let cool, then frost with vanilla buttercream.

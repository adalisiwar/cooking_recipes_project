Tunisian Mloukhiya Recipe

A rich and flavorful Tunisian stew made with ground Jew's mallow leaves, olive oil, and slow-cooked meat.

Ingredients

  • 100g ground Mloukhiya leaves (Jew's mallow powder)
  • 200ml olive oil
  • 500g beef or lamb, cut into medium pieces
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon caraway
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder (optional)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1.5 liters water

Instructions

1. In a large pot, mix the ground Mloukhiya leaves with olive oil over low heat until it forms a smooth paste.

2. Gradually add water while stirring to avoid lumps, and let it come to a gentle boil.

3. Add the meat pieces, minced garlic, tomato paste, and spices (coriander, caraway, chili powder, salt, and pepper).

4. Reduce the heat to low and let the stew simmer for 4-6 hours, stirring occasionally to prevent sticking.

5. Once the oil rises to the surface and the meat is tender, the Mloukhiya is ready.

6. Serve hot with crusty bread or over rice. Enjoy this comforting Tunisian classic!

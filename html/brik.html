Tunisian Brik Recipe

A crispy and flavorful pastry filled with tuna, eggs, and herbs, then fried to perfection.

Ingredients

  • 4 sheets of brik pastry (or filo pastry)
  • 4 eggs
  • 200g of tuna (canned or fresh)
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon of parsley, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon of harissa (optional for spiciness)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Instructions

1. In a bowl, mix the tuna, onion, parsley, harissa, salt, and pepper.

2. Spread a sheet of brik pastry on a flat surface. Place some of the filling in the center, then crack an egg on top of the filling.

3. Carefully fold the edges of the pastry to form a triangular shape, sealing the edges well.

4. Heat vegetable oil in a pan over medium heat. Fry the brik on both sides until golden and crispy.

5. Serve hot with a squeeze of lemon on top!

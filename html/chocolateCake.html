Chocolate Cake

A delicious chocolate cake recipe perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup butter

Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).

2. Mix dry ingredients in one bowl, and wet ingredients in another.

3. Combine the two mixtures and pour into a greased cake pan.

4. Bake for 30-35 minutes.

5. Let it cool before frosting.

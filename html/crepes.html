Classic Crepes Recipe

Thin, soft, and delicious crepes that can be filled with your favorite sweet or savory toppings.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 1 tbsp sugar (optional for sweet crepes)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract (optional)
  • 1 tbsp butter, melted
  • Pinch of salt
  • Butter or oil for frying

Instructions

1. In a bowl, whisk together the flour, eggs, milk, sugar, vanilla, and melted butter until smooth. Let the batter rest for 30 minutes.

2. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and lightly grease with butter or oil.

3. Pour a small amount of batter into the skillet and swirl to coat the bottom. Cook for 1-2 minutes until the edges begin to lift.

4. Flip the crepe and cook for another 30 seconds to 1 minute. Repeat with the remaining batter.

5. Serve with your favorite fillings, such as Nutella, fresh fruit, whipped cream, or sugar.

Tunisian Fricassé Recipe

A delicious fried sandwich filled with tuna, boiled eggs, and olives.

Ingredients

  • 4 small bread rolls
  • 200g tuna, drained
  • 2 boiled eggs, chopped
  • 50g olives, pitted and chopped
  • 1 tablespoon mayonnaise
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Instructions

1. In a bowl, mix tuna, boiled eggs, olives, mayonnaise, salt, and pepper.

2. Cut the bread rolls in half and fill them with the tuna mixture.

3. Heat oil in a pan and fry the filled rolls until golden brown.

4. Serve hot as a snack or appetizer.

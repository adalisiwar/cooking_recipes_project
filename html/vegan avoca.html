Vegan Avocado Toast Recipe

A simple yet delicious vegan breakfast of mashed avocado on toast with a sprinkle of salt, pepper, and lemon juice.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices whole grain bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chili flakes or sesame seeds for topping (optional)

Instructions

1. Toast the slices of bread until crispy.

2. In a bowl, mash the avocado and mix with lemon juice, salt, and pepper.

3. Spread the mashed avocado evenly on the toasted bread.

4. Optionally, top with chili flakes or sesame seeds for extra flavor.

5. Serve and enjoy your simple and delicious vegan avocado toast!

French Macarons

Light, crispy, and filled with your favorite ganache or buttercream.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 cup almond flour
  • 2 large egg whites
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Food coloring (optional)
  • Filling (buttercream, ganache, or jam)

Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 285°F (140°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

2. Sift powdered sugar and almond flour together into a bowl.

3. Beat egg whites until stiff peaks form, then slowly add granulated sugar.

4. Fold the almond flour mixture into the egg whites and pipe small circles onto the baking sheet.

5. Bake for 12-15 minutes and let cool before filling with ganache or buttercream.

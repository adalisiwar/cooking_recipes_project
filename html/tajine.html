Tunisian Tajine Recipe

A savory dish made with eggs, meat, and vegetables, baked into a delicious casserole.

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken or lamb, cut into pieces
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 zucchini, grated
  • 2 potatoes, boiled and sliced
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped parsley for garnish

Instructions

1. In a skillet, heat olive oil and brown the chicken or lamb pieces. Add the chopped onion and cook until soft.

2. Add the zucchini and cook for a few more minutes. Season with cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper.

3. In a large bowl, beat the eggs and mix with the vegetables and meat. Transfer the mixture to a greased baking dish.

4. Arrange the boiled potato slices on top of the mixture.

5. Bake in the oven at 180°C (350°F) for 30-40 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and the eggs are set.

6. Garnish with parsley and serve warm.

Cheesecake

Rich, creamy, and perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 4 packages (8 oz each) cream cheese
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • Fresh berries (optional, for topping)

Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 325°F (160°C). Mix graham cracker crumbs with melted butter and press into the bottom of a springform pan.

2. Beat cream cheese and sugar until smooth, then add vanilla and eggs one at a time, beating well.

3. Pour the mixture over the crust and bake for 55-60 minutes until the center is set.

4. Cool and chill in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours before serving. Top with fresh berries if desired.

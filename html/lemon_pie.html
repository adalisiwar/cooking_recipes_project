Lemon Pie

A tender and crumbly pastry or graham cracker crust serves as the base, providing a slightly sweet and buttery contrast to the tart lemon filling.

Ingredients

  • 1 package cream cheese, room temperature
  • 1 can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1/3 cup lemon juice
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 prepared graham cracker crust
  • 1/2 cup whipped cream for serving

Instructions

1. Mix together the cream cheese, milk and lemon juice.

2. Mix well and spread in graham crust

3. Chill until set, at least 2 hours.

4. top as desired, you can use lemon zest for decoration.Either way, a little Cool Whip or whipped cream on top is good.

5. Let it cool before frosting.

Tunisian Ojja Recipe

A spicy and flavorful dish made with tomatoes, eggs, and merguez sausage.

Ingredients

  • 4 merguez sausages
  • 4 eggs
  • 4 tomatoes (grated)
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 teaspoons harissa
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet. Cook the merguez sausages until browned, then set aside.

2. In the same skillet, saut√© the onion and garlic until fragrant.

3. Add the grated tomatoes, tomato paste, harissa, cumin, and paprika. Stir well and let the mixture simmer for 10 minutes.

4. Return the sausages to the skillet. Crack the eggs on top of the sauce, spacing them evenly.

5. Cover and cook on low heat until the eggs are set to your liking.

6. Serve hot with crusty bread for dipping.

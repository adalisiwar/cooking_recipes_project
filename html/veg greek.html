Vegan Greek Salad Recipe

A refreshing vegan twist on the classic Greek salad with tofu feta and a tangy dressing.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1/4 red onion, sliced
  • 1/2 cup Kalamata olives
  • 1/2 cup cubed tofu (marinated in lemon juice, olive oil, and oregano)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon red wine vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

1. In a large bowl, combine the cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, olives, and tofu feta.

2. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, red wine vinegar, oregano, salt, and pepper.

3. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss gently to coat.

4. Serve immediately or chill before serving for a cooler option.

Tunisian Lablabi Recipe

A comforting Tunisian dish made with chickpeas, garlic, and cumin, perfect for cold days.

Ingredients

  • 300g of chickpeas (soaked overnight)
  • 1 liter of water
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Stale bread (cut into pieces)
  • 1 egg per person
  • Tuna (canned)
  • Harissa to taste
  • Olive oil for serving

Instructions

1. Drain the soaked chickpeas and place them in a large pot with water.

2. Add garlic, cumin, salt, and pepper, and bring to a boil. Let it simmer until the chickpeas are tender (about 1 hour).

3. Meanwhile, prepare your bowls by placing pieces of stale bread at the bottom.

4. Once the chickpeas are cooked, pour the hot soup over the bread in each bowl.

5. Add a raw egg to the center of the bowl. The heat from the soup will lightly cook the egg.

6. Top with tuna, a spoonful of harissa, and a drizzle of olive oil. Serve hot.
